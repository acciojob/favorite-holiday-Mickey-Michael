Manali


Manali is one of the most beautiful hill stations in India. It is famous for its snow-covered mountains, pleasant weather, adventure sports, and breathtaking natural beauty. Every year, thousands of tourists visit Manali to enjoy their vacations.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Solang Valley - Famous for paragliding and skiing.
  2. Rohtang Pass - A beautiful mountain pass with snow.
  3. Hadimba Temple - An ancient temple.
  4. Mall Road - Best place for shopping.
  5. Jogini Waterfall - A beautiful waterfall.

Must-Try Foods

  • Siddu - Traditional Himachali dish.
  • Thukpa - Delicious noodle soup.
  • Momos - Popular street food.
  • Trout Fish - Local delicacy.
  • Babru - Famous Himachali snack.
